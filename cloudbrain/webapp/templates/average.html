Delta (0-4 Hz) waves are associated with very deep relaxation.

Theta (4-7 Hz) waves are associated with deep relaxation and calmness.

Alpha (7-13 Hz) waves occur when we are in a state of wakeful relaxation with closed eyes.

Beta (13-30 Hz) waves are emitted when we feel alert, agitated, tense, or afraid.

Gamma (30-70 Hz) waves are involved in cognitive processing and consolidation of information.
