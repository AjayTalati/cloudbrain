Can you understand why some brainwaves are more prominent than others in this exhibit?

 

Delta (0-4 Hz) Delta waves are associated with very deep relaxation.

Theta (4-7 Hz) Theta waves are associated with deep relaxation and calmness.

Alpha (7-13 Hz) Alpha waves occur when we are in a state of wakeful relaxation with closed eyes.

Beta (13-30 Hz) Beta waves are emitted when we feel alert, agitated, tense, or afraid.

Gamma (30-70 Hz) Gamma waves are involved in cognitive processing and consolidation of information.
